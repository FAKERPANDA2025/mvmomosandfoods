About Us

At MV Momos & Foods, we take pride in being the leading frozen momo wholesaler in Dewas, Indore, and Ujjain. As the only mass provider of frozen momos in these Dewas, we stand out for offering the highest quality momos at the most affordable rates, ensuring our customers get the best value. Unlike others operating from warehouses or homes, we have a dedicated factory designed for large-scale production, maintaining strict hygiene standards at every step. This commitment to cleanliness and care reflects our motto of "Quality, Purity, and Surety." Although we don’t rely on special recipes, our momos are crafted with a focus on taste and eco-friendliness, free from harmful chemicals. Our humble service and high standards have earned us the trust of our loyal customers. With stall services and a production setup built on efficiency, we have grown to become the biggest frozen momo wholesaler in the region. At MV Momos & Foods, we are dedicated to delivering excellence while supporting sustainability and creating a positive impact on the food culture of our cities.

Location: Main market, Sayanji Gate, Chopati Dewas.
